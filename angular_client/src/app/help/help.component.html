How to use our platform

How to create a project Create your own project and manage users

Create project

To create your own project, you'll have to click on "Project" page found in the menu aboove.

Step 1: Open the create project modal

By clicking on the "Create project" button right under the top of the page you will open a new model where you can create your project.

Step 2: Chose the name you want

The name you will chose will appear in the table of projects right after you confirm by clicking the "Confirm" button.

Step 3: Manage users

As an admin you will be able to manage the users you want to be part of your project. To do that, you'll have to press the "Manage users" button, at the botton of your project.

How to annotate an image Annotate your images step by step

Create project

First, you will have to be in one of your projects.

Step 1: Upload your images

In your project, you can upload your images by clicking on "Chose Image(.JPG)" button. You will be able to upload only .JPG images.

Step 2: Select your images and press the BIGIN ANNOTATION button

You can select the image/images you want to annotate by checking the "Select all" checkbox( this will select all your images) or by checkin on individual images one by one.

You also have the posibility to filter your images by name or by user.

Step 3: Chose your label

You can chose between one of the default labels our platform provides by clicking on the "Label list" accordion.

Step 4: Chose your drawing method

You will need to chose between 2 drawing methods: Polygon and Bounding Box. Chosing the polygon you will be able to drow more freely and chosing the boundig box this one will appear

on your image. You will be able to move it and resize it wharever you want.

Step 5: Save your work/ delete your annotations

After placing your bounding boxes/polygons (as in the picture below) you will be able to save your work by pressing the "Save annotation button. This button will save your work so you

won't lose it. If you want to delete an annotation, you only have to click right on it. If you want to delete all your annotations from the current imege, you can click the

"Clear selection" button.

Step 6: Add your own label

If you want to create your own labels you can easily do that by clicking on "Add new label" button. This will open a new modal window where you can write your new label's name. After

pressing the "Confirm" button you will find your new label in the labels list.

Step 7: Mark as verified

If you want to inform your colleges that the image you annotated is verified, all you have to do is check the "Frame verified" checkbox a verified icon will appear in the table of images.
